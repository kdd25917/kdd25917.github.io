Atlanta United

Atlanta United is a new Major league soccer team located in Atlanta Georgia. Atlanta united has had great success since becoming a team in 2014. This is brought by the introduction of our great players, and coach Gerardo Martino. Atlanta being a very diverse city brings out thousands of supporters. Atlanta United has awaken the state of Georgia with its flare, and ultimately the willingness to win.

With Atlanta United being owned by Arthur Blank, he allowed the fans to help in choosing team name, logo, and color scheme. These colors are red, black, and gold. The color red is for the passion, pride and resilience our team has. Black represents strength, and Gold for greatness committed by the club and fans. All these things are exactly what Atlanta United has experienced, shown, and delivered in their first two seasons.

In just two seasons Atlanta United dominance has been established with not only winning games but also by winning the 2018 MLS Cup. This revived the City of Atlanta, bringing back the flair our state needed. MLS also acknowledged Atlanta United forward Josef Martinez as MLS league MVP. This award is big considering the 672 possible candidates beginning the season.Not only was a player acknowledged. Atlanta United coach,Gerardo Martino was named coach of the year by the MLS.
